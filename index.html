About This Site

This is my project for a tech class that I'm taking. I'm sorry that the website isn't professional. I'm new to this whole thing, and, for that reason, this site is an experiment on my part.

I hope you enjoyed the cat pictures and were able to get at least somewhat of an idea of what breeds of cats are out there. If you are interested in any one of these breeds, feel free to look up different breeders online. I can say from experience that cats make exciting and wonderful pets!
